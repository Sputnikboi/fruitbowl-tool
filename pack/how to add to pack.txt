HOW TO ADD MODELS TO THE FRUITBOWL RESOURCE PACK
=================================================

Option 1: Fruitbowl Tool (Recommended)
---------------------------------------
Download the Fruitbowl Tool for an easy GUI that handles everything
for you — importing .bbmodel files, managing textures, dispatch
entries, and the model list.

  https://github.com/Sputnikboi/fruitbowl-tool

Video guide: (coming soon)


Option 2: Manual
-----------------
1. Export your model from Blockbench as a .bbmodel file.

2. Place your model JSON in:
     assets/fruitbowl/models/item/<model_name>.json

3. Place your texture PNG in:
     assets/fruitbowl/textures/item/<model_name>.png

4. Add a dispatch entry to the Minecraft item definition in:
     assets/minecraft/items/<item_type>.json
   with the appropriate custom_model_data threshold.

5. Create a Fruitbowl item definition in:
     assets/fruitbowl/items/<model_name>.json

6. Update "model list.txt" with the model name, threshold,
   and author under the correct heading.
